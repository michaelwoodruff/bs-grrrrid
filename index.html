Grid Bookmarklet

Drag the Grid button into your bookmarks.
Click the bookmark to display a semi-transparent grid overlay on any page.

Modifiers
Shift + Up/Down to increase/descrease opacity.
Shift + Right/Left to increase/decrease width.
Shift + 1 to reset width.
Shift + F to toggle fluid.
Esc to exit.

Ain't Working?
Please be aware that the bookmarklet won't load on sites that prevent script injection including Gmail and GitHub.
